What is Emmi?

Emmi is an intelligent voice assistant designed for institutions and organizations that handle high volumes of repetitive phone inquiries. It speaks naturally in both Romanian and Russian, providing consistent, accurate answers every time.

Powered by advanced speech recognition and AI reasoning, Emmi understands caller intent, delivers knowledge-base-driven responses, and automatically extracts structured data from every conversation for analytics and follow-up.

How It Works

From incoming call to structured insights — fully automated

01

Caller Dials In

Emmi picks up the call instantly, greets the caller in their language, and begins a natural conversation.

02

AI Understands & Responds

Real-time speech recognition transcribes the question. The AI reasons over the knowledge base and delivers an accurate spoken answer.

03

Data Is Extracted

After the call, Emmi automatically extracts caller details, topics discussed, and callback requests — ready for your team's review.
