Concept & Meaning

Legalia is a learning application dedicated to law students, designed to facilitate understanding of legal logic, not just memorizing information.

Through structured exercises, explanatory quizzes, and clear feedback, the application helps students correlate concepts, distinguish legal nuances, and consolidate their critical thinking.
