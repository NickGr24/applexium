Concept & Mission

Precedentia is a specialized legal search platform for the Republic of Moldova. It brings together jurisprudence from three essential sources into a single, intelligent interface.

The platform offers instant keyword search, AI-powered semantic search that understands context and legal reasoning, and an AI assistant that answers legal questions with exact citations from existing case law.

All databases are automatically updated from official sources, ensuring practitioners always have access to the latest decisions.

Platform Features

Three search modes for every legal research need

Keyword Search

Instant full-text search across all databases with advanced filters by source, date, court chamber, and document type

Semantic Search

Describe your legal situation in natural language. The AI finds relevant cases by understanding context, not just keywords

AI Legal Assistant

Ask legal questions and receive answers grounded in existing jurisprudence, with exact citations and case references

Advanced Filters
